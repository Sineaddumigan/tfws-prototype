Before you start

You will need to:

  • know the names and email addresses of everyone who needs access to your flood warnings account
  • have a file that lists the locations you need flood warnings for – a ShapeFile saved in ZIP format or an XLS spreadsheet
  • tell us how you want to receive flood warnings – by text, phonecall or email

Each location needs to be a full UK postcode.

There are different services if your organisation is in Wales or Scotland. These are run by Natural Resources Wales and Scotland Environment Protection Agency.
